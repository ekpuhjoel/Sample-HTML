THE GOLDEN HANDS BAKERY

The Journey So Far

"A journey of a thousand miles they say begins with a step in the right direction", this was the testimony of the golden hands bakery. for so long it had been a dream of the CEO and founder of the company Mr. Johnbull Ekpuh but exactly on the 17th of November 2019, this dream came to reallity.

Of corse like every other journey has its ups and downs, The Golden Hands bakery had it fair share of both, starting with the disappearance of one of its workers with a huge sum of money just few days after the inception of the company. Some would have thought that after such an occurence, the company would hit brick wall. Absolutely not! rather, the business took back its fit on a much more stronger foundation and since then only good testimonies have echoed out of there.
